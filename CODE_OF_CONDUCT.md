# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions
- Give and receive feedback professionally
- Focus discussions on technical content and community health

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or review processes

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and related community interactions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report violations, contact: security@rhineqc.com

All reports are reviewed confidentially and handled in good faith.
